What is the Magic Hate Ball?

The Magic Hate Ball is a project put together by a small band of Code Fellows students. We chose to make a twist on the traditional magic 8 ball to make it more interesting for users to engage. Our magic Hate Ball is a grumpy cat that is hard to please. Little do you know there is a secret to make this grumpy cat happy, and give you the answers you desire.
